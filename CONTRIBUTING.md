# CONTRIBUTING

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Intercognition. This repository is intended for documentation, metadata, and light collaborative coordination.

## Principles

- Respectful and courteous communication.
- Small, iterative contributions preferred.
- Transparency about provenance and licensing of proposed resources.

## How to contribute

1. Open an issue to propose a feature or report a problem.
2. For text changes or structured additions: submit a pull request (PR) against the `main` branch.
3. Use the provided issue and PR templates.

## Review process

- PRs will be reviewed by at least one maintainer.
- Non-technical contributions (text, metadata) may be merged after approval.

## Contribution license

By submitting a PR you agree to license your contribution under CC BY 4.0, unless explicitly stated otherwise.
